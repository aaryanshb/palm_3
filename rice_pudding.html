Creamy Rice Pudding

Anonymously submitted

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups cold water
  • ¾ cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups milk, divided
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • ⅔ cup golden raisins
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Pour water into a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat; stir in rice. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  2. Stir in the remaining 1/2 cup milk, beaten egg, and raisins; cook for 2 more minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla until combined.
  3. Serve warm.
